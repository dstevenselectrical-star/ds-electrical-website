Helpful, not required

What to mention (if it helps).

None of this is required — write whatever you want. But these four bits of info make a review genuinely useful for the next person reading it.

1

The work we did

EICR, EV charger install, fault-find, full rewire, kitchen lighting, CCTV — whatever it was, in your own words.

2

Your nearest town

Wells, Bath, Frome, Shepton, Street, Castle Cary, Glastonbury — gives local context for future readers.

3

How it went

Honest is best. On time? Tidy? Communicative? Anything we could have done better? All useful.

4

Photos (optional)

If you took any pictures of the install — consumer unit, EV point, finished kitchen — Google lets you attach them.

Privacy

We don't ask for or store any reviewer details ourselves. Reviews live publicly on Google, TrustMark or Checkatrade under your platform account — we just read them like everyone else. We don't offer discounts, vouchers or any incentive in exchange for a review (the CMA rightly bans that). All we'd like is your honest take.

Got a problem instead?

If something wasn't right, tell us first — we'd much rather fix it than read about it on Google. Phone or text Dan directly.
