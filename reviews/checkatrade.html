About Checkatrade

What Checkatrade actually checks

Checkatrade is a long-standing UK trade directory that vets every member before listing. The vetting includes identity confirmation, credit checks, public liability insurance verification, and references from prior customers.

Reviews on Checkatrade are submitted by paying customers after a job is completed and are tied back to a specific work record — they're harder to fake than free-form online reviews.
