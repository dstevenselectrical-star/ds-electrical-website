Domestic & Rewires

Full and partial rewires, consumer unit upgrades, kitchen and bathroom circuits, pendant lighting, socket additions, fault finding and extension work. Barn conversions, listed buildings and new-builds. Every job BS 7671 tested and NAPIT certified.

Commercial & Industrial

Three-phase supplies, main switch panels and sub-distribution boards, warehouse high-bay LED, control panels, machinery wiring, power factor correction, thermal imaging surveys and planned maintenance. CHAS accredited for contractor compliance.

CCTV, Alarms & Security

Hikvision 4K AcuSense and ColorVu cameras, NVR systems with remote Hik-Connect viewing, AX PRO wireless intruder alarms, access control and video entry. GDPR-compliant signage and commissioning included.

Data, Fibre & Office Fit-Outs

Cat6 and Cat6A structured cabling, fibre optic runs and terminations, Cisco Meraki Wi-Fi, comms cabinets, server rooms, Cat A and Cat B office fit-outs, small power layouts, lighting and HVAC interfaces.

EV Charger Installation

Hypervolt 3, Fastamps, Easee, Ohme, Zappi and Andersen. Dedicated 32A radial circuits, Type A RCD with 6mA DC detection, PME earthing surveys, DNO notification and OZEV grant paperwork handled end-to-end.

Lighting Design & Install

Residential, heritage and commercial schemes. LED strip, downlights, pendants, track, landscape and external lighting. Smart controls (Lutron, Rako, Casambi, DALI-2). BS 5266 emergency lighting and BS 5839 fire alarm integration.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does a full rewire cost?+

A typical 3-bed house rewire starts from around £3,500. Price depends on the number of circuits, accessibility, and whether it includes a consumer unit upgrade. We provide free, no-obligation quotes with a full breakdown.

Do I need an EICR for my rental property?+

Yes. Since April 2021, all landlords in England must have a valid EICR before new tenancies and at least every 5 years. We can test and certify your property, usually within 24 hours of booking.

Are you insured?+

Yes. We carry £2 million public liability insurance and all our work is guaranteed. We're also NAPIT approved, CHAS accredited, and TrustMark endorsed.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?+

We aim to respond to all emergency callouts within 2 hours across our Mid Somerset coverage area. For non-urgent enquiries, we get back to you the same day.

What areas do you cover?+

We're based in Glastonbury and cover all of Mid Somerset including Wells, Shepton Mallet, Bath, Frome, Street, Midsomer Norton, Bruton, Castle Cary, Radstock, and Wedmore.
