Local Knowledge

We know Midsomer Norton like our own back garden.

Midsomer Norton has the housing stock of an ex-mining town and the demands of a modern commuter community. Victorian terraces, post-war semis, and growing new-build estates all within a few miles of the High Street — each with different wiring ages and upgrade needs.

Streets, neighbourhoods and villages we cover

We regularly work in the High Street and South Road, Welton, Westfield, Paulton, Chilcompton, Holcombe, Norton Hill, Peasedown St John and Stratton-on-the-Fosse.

Typical work in Midsomer Norton

Consumer unit upgrades on Victorian terraces with rubber-insulated legacy wiring, full rewires, new-build snagging and first-fix work on the growing estates around Welton and Westfield, commercial EICRs for the industrial estates, EV chargers.

Housing stock

Victorian miners' cottages and terraces, 1930s-60s semis, ex-council estates, post-2010 developer estates, and light industrial units along the A362.
