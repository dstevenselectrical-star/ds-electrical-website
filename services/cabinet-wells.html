Common Questions

Do you cover the schools and surgeries around Wells?

Yes — Wells Cathedral School area, the surgeries on Glastonbury Road, and the high street retail strip are all in our regular install patch. Wells is roughly 20 minutes from our Glastonbury base via the A39, so we’re on site quickly for surveys and follow-up calls.

What size cabinet do I need — wall-mount or floor-standing?

Depends on rack-unit count. A small office (1 patch panel + 1 PoE switch + UPS) needs a 9U or 12U wall-mount. A school floor or surgery needs a 15U or 18U wall-mount. Anything with multiple switches, UPS, NVR and patching usually wants a 27U or 42U floor-standing. We size on site and quote both options where it makes sense.

Do you install lockable cabinets and structured cabling labels?

Always. Every commercial cabinet we install is lockable (key or combination), every patch lead is colour-coded by VLAN, every port is labelled at both ends, and we hand over a cabinet schedule PDF showing patch panel port → wall port → room. This makes fault-finding painless and the next contractor can read the system without us.
