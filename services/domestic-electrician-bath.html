Local Knowledge · Bath

Why domestic electrical work in Bath is different

Bath's domestic stock is mostly Georgian and Victorian — Bath stone terraces, listed Crescents (Royal Crescent, The Circus, Lansdown), basement conversions and high-end period renovations. The bulk of our domestic Bath work is full and partial heritage rewires, EV charger installs on hill-street parking with cable routing challenges, lighting design retrofits with concealed routing, and consumer unit upgrades on older 1970s/1980s split-load boards that no longer comply with BS 7671 18th edition for new EV or hot tub circuits.

Listed-building consent matters in central Bath — we use brass / pewter conduit and Click Scolmore Deco / Deco Plus screwless metallic accessories on heritage rewires, with concealed routing under floorboards where consent allows. Basement conversions need RCBO-protected sub-mains and damp-rated downlights.
