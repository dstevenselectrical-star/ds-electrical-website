Are you the only OZEV-approved EV installer in Glastonbury?

No, but we’re local — based in Glastonbury, registered at a BA6 address. We’re NAPIT-registered and OZEV-authorised. Most Glastonbury EV jobs are domestic Hypervolt or Zappi installs taking half a day, including the DNO notification and OZEV grant paperwork.

Do you fit Type A RCBO on every EV charger install?

Yes — always. BS 7671 18th edition (Amendment 2) requires Type A or Type B RCBO on EV charger circuits to detect DC fault current. We use Type A by default (correct for all EV chargers we install) — never Type AC. This is non-negotiable.

Which EV charger brands do you install?

Hypervolt, Zappi (myenergi), Ohme, Andersen, Project EV, Wallbox.
