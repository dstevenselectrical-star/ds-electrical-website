FAQ

Common questions

Do you do out-of-hours commercial work in Glastonbury?

Yes. A lot of our Glastonbury commercial work is done outside trading hours — early starts before retail opens, late finishes after offices empty, full overnight shifts where the business can't shut down for the day. We quote out-of-hours rates upfront so there are no surprises, and we have the spare capacity to cover both Dans on a single overnight if scope demands it.

Are you CHAS accredited and what does that cover?

Yes — DS Electrical is CHAS-accredited, which is the standard pre-qualification used by managing agents, councils and main contractors. It covers our health-and-safety policies, RAMS, insurance, training records and competency. We're also NAPIT-approved (electrical) and TrustMark-endorsed. Documentation pack on request.

Do you handle commercial 5-yearly EICRs and remedials?

Yes — landlord and commercial EICRs are core work. We test, issue the certificate, schedule the C1/C2 remedials with the tenant, and re-issue a satisfactory certificate once works are done. We also keep a remedial register so future inspections start from a known baseline.
