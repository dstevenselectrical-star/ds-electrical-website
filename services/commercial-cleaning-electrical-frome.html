Local Knowledge · Frome

Why commercial cleaning & depot work in Frome is different

Frome commercial cleaning work clusters around Marston Trading Estate, Commerce Park and the Wallbridge industrial belt — vehicle workshops, contract cleaning depots, fleet washdown bays for the local logistics operators. The trading estates have plenty of 3-phase headroom but circuits are often a 1980s patchwork with shared RCDs that don't meet 18th edition for new wash-bay or fleet EV work.

Catherine Hill / Market Yard contract cleaners (Frome's independent retail strip) need IP65 outdoor sockets on dedicated RCBO circuits — older shopfronts share lighting and small power on one MCB which fails for outdoor pressure-washer and fleet-charger use. We board-change, run the dedicated outdoor circuit, and certify.
