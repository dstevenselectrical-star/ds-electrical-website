What's the difference between PoE+, PoE++ and PoE?

PoE (802.3af) gives 15.4 W per port — enough for a basic AP or VoIP phone. PoE+ (802.3at) gives 30 W — standard for most commercial APs, IP cameras and access-control kit. PoE++ (802.3bt) gives up to 90 W — needed for multi-radio outdoor APs, heated cameras, or PTZ domes. We size the switch to your kit list.

Do you install managed or unmanaged switches?

For commercial sites we always install managed switches — you need VLANs to separate staff / guest / CCTV / IoT traffic, and you need the management interface for fault-finding. Reyee RG-NBS3100 series is our default (24-port and 48-port PoE+ variants, lifetime cloud management free, no licence renewal). Unmanaged is fine for a domestic AP — not for a commercial install.
