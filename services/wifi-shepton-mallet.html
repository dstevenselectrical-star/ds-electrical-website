FAQ

Common Questions

Do you install commercial Wi-Fi at Royal Bath & West showground sites or Shepton industrial units?

Yes. The Royal Bath & West site, the market square retail, and the industrial units off Cannards Grave Road are all in our patch. Outdoor IP67 APs cover yards and showground perimeters where indoor-only kit would fail. Shepton is about 15 minutes from our Glastonbury base.

Which Wi-Fi access points do you fit?

We default to Reyee (the SME range from Ruijie). Reyee Cloud is free for life, the AP hardware is roughly a third of UniFi pricing for equivalent specs, and the IP67 outdoor variants handle UK weather without enclosures. For sites that already run UniFi, Meraki or Omada we install those too — we’re kit-agnostic, the survey decides.

Do you fit outdoor APs for car parks, yards or showground sites?

Yes — Reyee RG-RAP6260 IP67 outdoor APs are our default for outdoor coverage. They handle −30°C to +65°C, are surge-protected on ethernet, and we mount them with proper drip loops and weatherproof glands. Powered over PoE+ from the same back-haul switch as the indoor kit.
