Fire Alarm Systems in Bruton
Built to BS 5839

Every fire alarm system we install in Bruton meets BS 5839-1 (commercial) or BS 5839-6 (domestic). Bruton has become one of Somerset's most desirable towns, with high-end restaurants, galleries and renovated period properties. Each of these buildings has specific fire safety obligations, and we design systems that satisfy compliance without compromising architectural character.

BS 5839 Categories Explained

  • Category L1 — Full property coverage. Every room protected. Required for care homes and high-risk premises
  • Category L2 — Escape routes plus high-risk rooms. Common for Bruton restaurants, galleries and commercial premises
  • Category L3 — Escape route protection only. Standard for most rental properties and landlord compliance
  • Category L4 — Circulation spaces only (hallways and landings). Minimum for single-occupancy dwellings

Conventional vs Addressable Systems

Smaller Bruton properties suit conventional zone-based panels at lower cost. For larger buildings such as restaurants, hotels and multi-unit commercial premises in Bruton, addressable systems identify the exact detector that triggered, reducing false alarm disruption and enabling targeted evacuation response.
