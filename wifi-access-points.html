How We Build It

Designed off a survey.
Cabled to spec.
Tested on the way out.

A commercial Wi-Fi job done badly is a forever-headache. We do it once: proper site survey first, Cat6a back-haul on PoE, the right number of access points in the right places, separate VLANs for staff / guest / IoT, and a test pack pack at handover.

01 Site Survey First

Floor plan, construction types (stone walls and foil-backed plasterboard kill 5 GHz), known interference sources, planned device density and throughput.

You get a heat-map of predicted signal at each AP, a bill of materials, and a fixed-price quote. Nothing on a guess.

02 Indoor PoE Access Points

Ceiling-mount Wi-Fi 6 access points back-hauled over Cat6a to a central PoE+ switch. Single SSID across the whole site, seamless roaming as users walk, separate guest network on its own VLAN.

Reyee RAP series default; UniFi, Omada or Meraki on request.

03 Outdoor & IP67 Access Points

For car parks, school grounds, hospital courtyards, factory yards, holiday park pitches and outdoor coverage. IP67 weatherproof rating, mounted on poles, walls or building corners with weatherproof Cat6a back-haul and proper surge protection.

04 Cabling Back-Haul (Cat6a)

Every AP needs a cable. We run Cat6a (10 Gbps to 100 m) as the default to future-proof for Wi-Fi 6E / 7. LSZH-sheathed for commercial, in containment, dressed and labelled.

Every link wire-mapped and continuity tested to TIA-568 Cat 6a permanent-link limits. PDF report per port at handover.

05 PoE+ & PoE++ Switching

Managed switches sized for the AP count and growth headroom. PoE+ (30 W per port) for most APs, PoE++ (60-90 W) for higher-power outdoor or Wi-Fi 7 kit. VLAN-capable for staff / guest / IoT separation on day one.

06 Free Cloud Controller

Reyee Cloud (and Ruijie Cloud) is free, no per-AP licence and no subscription. Manage the entire site from a browser or phone — see who’s online, change SSIDs, push firmware, run diagnostics.

Self-hosted UniFi controller and TP-Link Omada controller are also free options. Cisco Meraki is licence-based and we’ll price that in clearly if it’s preferred.

07 Guest, Staff & IoT VLANs

Three SSIDs from the same access points: Staff on its own VLAN with full access; Guest with captive-portal sign-in, content filter, bandwidth shaping and isolation from the staff network; IoT/CCTV on a third VLAN with no internet access if you don’t want it.

08 Migration Without Downtime

Got existing Wi-Fi? We install the new APs in parallel, verify coverage and throughput on a separate SSID, then cut over in a planned short window — usually out of hours for commercial sites. Staff and customers see no interruption.
