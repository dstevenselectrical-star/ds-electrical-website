What Every Contract Covers

Full Compliance, Fully Managed

BS 7671 Compliance

Every inspection and test follows the 18th Edition wiring regulations. We keep your installation legal and your insurance valid.

EICR Testing

Periodic inspection and testing with full condition reports. C1, C2, C3 and FI observations coded and explained in plain English.

Emergency Lighting (BS 5266)

Monthly flick tests, annual 3-hour duration tests. All results logged and certificates issued for your compliance files.

Fire Alarm Servicing (BS 5839)

Quarterly inspections, annual service visits. Zone testing, detector checks, battery condition, sounders verified across all zones.

PAT Testing

Risk-based portable appliance testing. Every item labelled, logged, and certificated. Failed items identified and replaced.

Thermal Imaging

Infrared scans of distribution boards and high-load connections. Identifies loose terminals, overloaded circuits, and hot spots before they fail.
