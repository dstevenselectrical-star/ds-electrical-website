Profile / Director

Dan Stevens

Director & Lead Electrician

"I started on industrial sites in the 90s. Now I run DS Electrical from Glastonbury covering Bath, Wells, Shepton, Frome, Bruton and the surrounding villages. Every job I price personally. Every cert I sign. No call-centres."

Three decades on the tools across industrial, commercial and domestic. The early years were heavy industry — three-phase distribution, motor circuits, factory maintenance — the kind of work that teaches you to test before you trust. The last decade has been a mix of heritage rewires on listed Somerset cottages, EV charging installs, BS 5839-1 fire alarm systems, and the EICRs that keep landlords compliant under MEES regulations.

I run the company. I price the work. I do most of it myself, and the rest goes through engineers I’ve worked with for years. You will never get handed off to a call-centre or a subcontractor you didn’t agree to.

What you get

Hire DS Electrical, get this.

No surprises. No upsell after the quote. No subcontractor stitch-ups.

01

Same engineer, quote to cert

The director who quotes the job is the engineer on site, and the same person signs the certificate at the end. No handoffs.

02

Free survey before pricing

We come out, look at it, and price it from what we’ve actually seen — not a guess off a phone call. Survey costs you nothing.

03

Plain-English itemised quote

Every quote breaks down labour, materials, certification and VAT separately. You can see exactly where your money goes.

04

Part P notified at no extra cost

NAPIT 66245 means we self-certify. Building Regulations Compliance Certificate posted to you within 30 days — no Building Control fee.

05

6-year workmanship warranty

Backed by NAPIT’s Insurance Backed Guarantee. If something we’ve installed fails on workmanship, it’s covered for six years.

06

£2m public liability

Full insurance cover including employer’s liability. CHAS-audited annually. Certificate available on request before any job starts.
