What we wire in your Shepton Mallet kitchen

01 / Cooker

Single oven 32 A circuit

Dedicated 6 mm² feed back to the consumer unit, RCBO protected, with a 45 A cooker switch above the worktop and a connection unit behind the appliance.

02 / Induction

Induction hob 32 A

Standalone 32 A circuit (induction draws hard at boost) — separated from the oven so they can run together without nuisance trips.

03 / Lighting

LED downlights & under-cabinet

Fire-rated dimmable LED downlights laid out for actual task light over worktops (not just ceiling symmetry), plus under-cabinet LED strip on its own switch.

04 / Sockets

Worktop ring main + USB

Click Deco screwless sockets along the splashback — chrome, brass, brushed steel or matt black to match your kitchen — with USB-A/USB-C twin sockets where you actually charge phones.

05 / Appliances

Integrated appliance isolation

Switched fused spurs above the worktop for dishwasher, washing machine, fridge-freezer, wine cooler — so you can isolate without pulling units out.

06 / Island

Kitchen island feed

Floor-buried armoured feed under the slab to a pop-up socket tower or end-panel sockets — first-fix only viable before the floor goes down.

07 / Wet

Dishwasher / washer RCBO

30 mA RCBO per appliance — Type A (DC-detecting) so modern inverter motors don't trick the trip.

08 / Extract

Extractor fan supply

Dedicated fused supply for cooker hood / external extract — switched live from the hob switch so it can interlock with the cooking.
