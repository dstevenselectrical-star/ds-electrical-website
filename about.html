Our Story

Who We Are

DS Electrical Installation SW Ltd is a professional electrical contracting business based in Glastonbury, Somerset, serving domestic and commercial clients across Mid Somerset and the wider South West.

Run by directors Dan Stevens and Dan Street, the company has over 15 years of experience in the electrical industry covering everything from full property rewires and consumer unit upgrades to EV charger installation, CCTV systems, commercial fit-outs, and agricultural installations.

Every job is completed to BS 7671 standards, fully tested, and certified. We are NAPIT approved, CHAS accredited, and TrustMark endorsed.

We're not a call centre. When you ring our number, you speak directly to the person who'll be doing the work. That means clear communication, honest pricing, and a genuine interest in getting the job done right.

Qualifications &
Accreditations

NAPIT Approved

Full scope domestic installer, competent person scheme. All work self-certified and notified to Building Control.

CHAS Accredited

Contractors Health and Safety Assessment Scheme. Required for commercial and public sector work.

City & Guilds 2391

Inspection and Testing of Electrical Installations. The industry standard for testing competence.

City & Guilds 2382

18th Edition Wiring Regulations (BS 7671:2018 + Amendment 2). Current regulatory standard.

City & Guilds 2365

Electrical Installation. The core qualification for professional electricians.

Part P Registered

Registered with a competent person scheme for domestic electrical work. Building Regulations compliant.

TrustMark

Government-endorsed quality scheme for tradespeople. Provides consumer protection and dispute resolution.

Round-the-clock emergency electrician response across Mid Somerset for contract clients and critical faults.
