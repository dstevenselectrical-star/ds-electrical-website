Domestic Services

Everything your
home needs.

From a single socket change to a full 4-bed rewire, we handle all domestic electrical work in Mid Somerset. Every job certified to BS 7671:2018 Amendment 2, every circuit tested, NAPIT Part P notification lodged on your behalf. No day rates, no surprises, no call centre.

01 Full and Partial Rewires

New consumer unit, fresh circuits, kitchen and bathroom wiring, smoke and heat detection to BS 5839-6, earthing and bonding. 2-bed flat from £3,500, 3-bed semi from £4,500, 4-bed detached from £6,500. Partial rewires (first floor only, kitchen only, etc.) quoted per scope.

02 Consumer Unit Upgrades

Hager, Schneider, Wylex and FuseBox metal consumer units with full RCBO protection and SPD (surge protection). Compliant with BS 7671:2018+A2 Amendment 2. From £650 for a typical domestic swap, installed in 3-4 hours with minimal disruption.

03 Kitchen & Bathroom Circuits

Dedicated circuits for oven, hob, shower, extractor, cooker hood, under-cabinet lighting, bathroom zones (IP-rated). Discreet cable runs coordinated with your plasterer and kitchen fitter. Certified to BS 7671.

04 Lighting Installation

LED downlights, pendants, wall lights, under-cabinet strip, landscape and external lighting. We supply and install from Astro, Nordlux, Collingwood and Integral. Dimmable circuits matched to the driver and dimmer. Heritage and conservation work a speciality.

05 EV Charger Installation

Hypervolt 3, Fastamps, Easee, Ohme, Zappi and Andersen. Dedicated 32A radial, Type A RCD with 6mA DC protection, PME earthing assessment, DNO notification, OZEV grant paperwork (up to £350 off). Typically installed in 2-4 hours.

06 Fault Finding & Repairs

Tripping RCDs, dead circuits, overheating sockets, failed lighting, nuisance trips under load. We bring the proper kit (Megger MFT, Fluke clamp meter, thermal imaging) and actually diagnose the root cause rather than guess. No fault-finding fix is quoted until we know what is wrong.

07 EICR for Homeowners

Home inspections when buying, selling, or you just want to know what state your installation is in. 3-bed domestic EICR from £180 including certificate. Satisfactory reports issued same-day. Unsatisfactory reports come with an itemised remedial quote.

08 Landlord EICR

Legally required every 5 years or at change of tenancy for rental properties. We liaise direct with tenants, issue certificates within 28 days, and handle remedial works if the report comes back unsatisfactory. Portfolio discounts for 5+ properties.

09 Smoke & Heat Alarms

Grade D mains-powered interlinked smoke and heat detectors (Aico, FireAngel, Kidde) with 10-year sealed battery backup. Required in all rented properties and strongly recommended in owner-occupied homes. Per the Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Alarm (Amendment) Regulations 2022.

10 Heritage & Listed Buildings

Mid Somerset is full of Listed cottages, Georgian townhouses, and barn conversions. We work around lath-and-plaster ceilings, ashlar stone walls, low beams, and liaise with conservation officers when Listed Building Consent is required. Wireless smoke alarms, discreet smart lighting, minimal-chase cable routing.
