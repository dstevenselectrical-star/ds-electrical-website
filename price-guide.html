Important: Prices Are Guide Only

Every property is different. These are typical price ranges to help you budget. Your actual quote may vary based on:

  • Property age and construction — period properties with lath and plaster cost more
  • Access — loft access, under-floor voids, or solid floors affect cable routing
  • Cable run length — longer distances increase materials and labour
  • Existing installation condition — additional remedial work may be needed
  • Specification — premium switches, smart controls, and high-end fittings

Contact us for an accurate quote based on your specific property and requirements.
