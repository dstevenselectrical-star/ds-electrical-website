What's included

  • Full consumer unit inspection — age, type, condition, compliance with 18th Edition

Signs you need this

  • Old fuseboxes with rewireable fuses (wire you replace) — pre-2000s installation
  • Plastic board with no main switch — fire risk per 18th Edition
  • RCDs tripping repeatedly — something's leaking to earth
