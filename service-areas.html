Coverage Principle

Local enough to be there fast. Skilled enough to do the work properly.

DS Electrical is based in Glastonbury (BA6). We work to a strict 40-minute drive radius from base — that keeps response times short, callout costs honest, and means we're not stretched thin on jobs we can't get back to. Wells

BA5
15 min · 6 mi

England's smallest city — cathedral close, Vicars' Close, Tor Street terraces and Georgian merchant houses on the Liberty.
