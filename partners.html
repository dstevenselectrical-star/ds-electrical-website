Brands & Manufacturers

What we install

We’re not tied to any one supplier. We specify and install the equipment that’s right for the job and the budget — from premium domestic charging to industrial three-phase distribution. Here’s what’s in our regular kit list.

01

EV Charging

Hypervolt 3

British-designed tube charger with customisable LEDs, OCPP, OTA updates, Octopus tariff integration.

Authorised installer

Fastamps

Heavy-duty commercial AC chargers, 7.4kW or 22kW three-phase, OCPP, RFID, MID metering. Workplace and fleet.

Commercial & fleet

Easee

Norwegian compact 7kW/22kW with built-in load balancing for up to 3 chargers on one supply. IP55, 3-yr warranty.

Ohme

Smart-tariff chargers (Octopus Agile, Go, Intelligent Go). Charges only when electricity is cheapest. CT-clamp ready.

Zappi (myenergi)

British-made charger with Fast, Eco and Eco+ modes. Part of the myenergi ecosystem, pairs with Eddi and Harvi.

Andersen

Premium British charger with real wood or painted metal fascia, 14 colour options. PEN fault detection built in.

02

CCTV & Security

Hikvision

4K AcuSense, ColorVu night vision, NVR systems, AX PRO wireless intruder alarms. Hik-Connect cloud viewing.

Accredited installer

Paxton

Net2 access control, Net10 cloud-based access, video entry. Scalable from single-door to multi-site.

2N

IP video intercoms with mobile-app answering, RFID and Bluetooth keyless entry, weather-rated outdoor stations.

03

Fire Alarm & Detection

Hochiki

EKHO wireless and conventional fire alarm systems. ESP intelligent open protocol. UK manufactured.

Apollo

XP95 / Discovery / Soteria addressable detection plus MultiPoint Pro wireless. Used by majors across the UK.

Advanced

MxPro 5 multi-loop addressable fire panels for medium and large commercial premises. UK designed.

Kentec

Syncro AS addressable fire panels. Robust, well-supported, common spec on UK commercial schemes.

Aico

Domestic and HMO smoke / heat / CO detectors. RadioLINK+ wireless interlinking. SMART-LINK app reporting.

FireAngel

Wi-Safe2 wireless interlinked smoke and heat alarms for landlord and HMO compliance.

04

Lighting Controls

Lutron

Caseta wireless and RA2 Select for residential and small commercial scene control. Award-winning hardware.

Rako

British-designed wired and wireless scene control. Strong on listed and period buildings where retrofit matters.

Casambi

Bluetooth mesh lighting control. No hub required, app-based commissioning, ideal for refit projects.

DALI-2

Open-protocol broadcast lighting control for commercial schemes. Vendor-agnostic, future-proof.

Loxone

Whole-home automation with hardware logic. Lighting + heating + audio + security in one ecosystem.

Shelly

Cost-effective Wi-Fi smart relays and dimmers. Local API, no cloud lock-in, integrates with everything.

05

Lighting Brands

Astro Lighting

British architectural and decorative lighting. Bathroom, wall, pendant, exterior. Premium fittings, IP-rated where it matters.

Erco

German precision track and architectural lighting. Retail, gallery and high-end residential.

Collingwood

British LED downlights, strip, and exterior. CRI90+ tape, fire-rated downlights, dimmable drivers.

Nordlux

Danish design-led residential and commercial lighting. Track, pendants, wall, exterior, smart-ready.

Hunza

New Zealand machined-bronze landscape lighting. Lifetime guarantee, 24V DC low voltage. The benchmark for outdoor.

Eaton

Emergency lighting, exit signs, central battery systems. BS 5266 compliant self-test units.

06

Consumer Units & Distribution

Hager

Domestic and commercial consumer units, distribution boards, RCBOs, MCBs. UK-manufactured at Telford.

Schneider Electric

Acti9 commercial distribution, Easy9 / KQ domestic boards. Power monitoring, surge protection, fault current limiters.

Wylex

Domestic consumer units, RCBOs, surge protection. UK manufactured, widely stocked, easy spares.

FuseBox

Cost-effective domestic consumer units with full RCBO protection and SPD as standard.

Test & Inspection Kit

Megger MFT

MFT1741 / MFT1845 multifunction testers. Insulation, continuity, RCD, earth fault loop, PSCC. Calibrated annually.

Fluke

Voltage indicators, clamp meters, thermal imaging cameras. The reference for live-circuit diagnostics.

Martindale

UK-designed lockout / tagout, voltage proving units, professional voltage indicators (HSE-compliant).
