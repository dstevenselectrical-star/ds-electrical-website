Common Questions

CCTV FAQ

How much does a CCTV system cost?+

A 4-camera Hikvision 4K system starts from around £1,200 fully installed, including the NVR recorder, cabling, and Hik-Connect remote viewing setup. Larger systems with 8+ cameras or PTZ units cost more — we provide a free site survey and fixed-price quote.

IP or analogue — which is better?+

We install IP (network) cameras exclusively. They offer 4K+ resolution, smart features like AcuSense person/vehicle detection, and remote viewing. Analogue systems are outdated and can't match the image quality or smart detection of modern IP cameras.

Can I view my cameras on my phone?+

Yes. We set up Hik-Connect on your phone during installation. You can view live feeds, receive motion alerts, and playback recordings from anywhere with an internet connection. Works on iPhone and Android.

Do I need planning permission for CCTV?+

Domestic CCTV generally doesn't need planning permission. However, you must comply with GDPR if cameras capture areas beyond your property boundary. We provide GDPR-compliant signage as standard and advise on camera positioning.

How long is CCTV footage stored?+

Typically 2–4 weeks depending on the number of cameras and hard drive size. We usually specify a 2TB or 4TB NVR. Motion-only recording extends storage significantly compared to continuous recording.
